Five dimensions. One transparent verdict.

Each round is evaluated across Logic (30%), Evidence (20%), Clarity (20%), Rebuttal (20%), and Rhetoric (10%). Gates prevent inflated scores when critical failures occur.

New users start with provisional ratings and high uncertainty — so you can climb quickly to your true bracket without artificial sandbagging.
